# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources.
- Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts.
- Think before sharing personal information such as your college ID, address, or phone number online.
- Verify job offers, internships, and scholarship messages before responding to avoid scams.

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
- Always double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before making payments.
- Do not approve “collect requests” unless you are sure about the sender.
- Avoid installing unknown apps that ask for financial permissions.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud cases.
