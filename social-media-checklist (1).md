# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post – Make sure your content is appropriate and won’t harm your reputation.  
- Maintain privacy settings – Regularly check and update who can see your posts and personal information.  
- Be respectful and positive – Communicate politely and avoid offensive language or behavior.  
- Verify information before sharing – Ensure news or facts are accurate to avoid spreading misinformation.  
- Build a professional presence – Share achievements, skills, and useful content that reflects your personality positively.  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can harm your image and escalate conflicts.  
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – This can affect your academic and career opportunities.  
- Don’t overshare your daily activities – It can compromise your privacy and safety.  
- Don’t blindly trust everything online – Be cautious of scams, fake profiles, and misleading content.
